Frequently Asked Questions

What is CourzGen?

CourzGen is a platform that helps you manage and grow your community by offering tools for memberships, event bookings, payments, and more.

How can I register for CourzGen?

You can register by clicking the "Register" button in the Hero section, and filling out the necessary details in the registration form.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial so you can explore our features before committing to a subscription.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

What types of payments are accepted?

We accept payments through all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
